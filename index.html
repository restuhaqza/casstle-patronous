The Story

A name, a place, and a promise.

The Patronous Project is named after the Patronus Charm — a defensive spell that takes the form of an animal guardian, unique to each witch or wizard. Like a Patronus, each AI agent is a protector in its domain: Dumbledore guards the big picture, Hermione guards knowledge, and Snape guards the walls.

The Harry Potter theme isn't just for fun — it reflects how the system works. Like Hogwarts houses, each agent has its own character and strengths. Like the Headmaster, Dumbledore coordinates without micromanaging. And like the castle itself, the system is always there when you need it.

This is a living project. Agents will come online as the ecosystem grows — from the current staff of three to a full castle of specialists. The magic is just getting started.
